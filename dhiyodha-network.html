Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dhiyodha Network?

Dhiyodha Network is a women-focused business networking community designed for entrepreneurs, founders, professionals, and business leaders to connect, collaborate, and grow through curated networking opportunities and events.

Who can join Dhiyodha Network?

Dhiyodha is open to women entrepreneurs, startup founders, CEOs, consultants, creators, and professionals across industries. Membership is application-based to maintain a trusted and high-quality community.

How do I apply for Dhiyodha membership?

You can apply by submitting your profile through the membership application form. Our team reviews applications and typically responds within a few business days.

Does Dhiyodha host networking events?

Yes. Dhiyodha regularly hosts networking meetups, founder events, workshops, business discussions, and community gatherings across multiple cities.

Is Dhiyodha available in my city?

Dhiyodha has active networking communities in multiple cities and continues expanding through local chapters and events.

How is Dhiyodha different from LinkedIn or Facebook groups?

Unlike generic networking platforms, Dhiyodha focuses on meaningful women-first networking, verified members, real-world events, collaborations, and long-term professional relationships.
