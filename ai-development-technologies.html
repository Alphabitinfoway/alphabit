Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Everything you need to know about AI development and how we implement it.

What is AI development?

AI development involves building systems that simulate human intelligence using machine learning, NLP, and other AI technologies.

What technologies are used in AI development?

Common technologies include TensorFlow, PyTorch, NLP frameworks, computer vision tools, and cloud platforms.

What is the difference between AI and machine learning?

AI is the broader concept of intelligent systems, while machine learning is a subset that focuses on learning from data.

What is an AI tech stack?

It includes programming languages, frameworks, cloud platforms, and tools used to build and deploy AI systems.

What are real-world applications of AI?

Chatbots, recommendation systems, fraud detection, image recognition, and predictive analytics.

How long does AI development take?

It depends on complexity, data availability, and use case — typically ranging from weeks to months.

What is MLOps in AI?

MLOps is the practice of managing the AI lifecycle, including deployment, monitoring, and continuous improvement.

Why is data important in AI?

High-quality data is essential for training accurate and reliable AI models.
