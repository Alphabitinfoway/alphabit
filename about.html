Alphabit Infoway began its journey on March 8, 2018, with a vision to bring innovation to the education sector through technology.

Founded by Poonam Tala, a Computer Engineering graduate from Gujarat Technological University, the company started as a small software development company with a team of three developers in a classroom setup.

Today, Alphabit has grown into a global AI development company and IT solutions provider, delivering custom software development, product solutions, and training.
