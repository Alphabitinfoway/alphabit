Alphabit Infoway had started its journey on March 8, 2018 at Women's Day. Poonam Tala, The founder of Alphabit, had a single vision, to bring innovation in the education field with the help of information technology.

After completing her computer engineering at Gujarat Technological University (GTU), Poonam Tala started Alphabet with three developers by setting up ten computers in a classroom of her relative's school to make her vision worthwhile.

Today, along with industrial training, Alphabit provides small scale to large scale industrial services and also does product development on the unique ideas of entrepreneur's and with its branches in two mega cities of Gujarat, Alphabit also provides services with its team in countries like Dubai, Denmark including US, UK, Australia and Canada.
