About DhiSure

DhiSure is designed specifically for vehicle insurance brokers who need to manage client and agent data efficiently — without the chaos of spreadsheets or disconnected tools.

With a clean, unified workspace and support for multiple assistants, DhiSure helps brokerages operate faster, more accurately, and with far less manual effort.

Key Capabilities:

Unified Client & Agent Data Management
Multiple Assistant Account Support
Vehicle Policy & Renewal Tracking
Smart Organisation & Search Tools
Role-Based Access for Your Entire Team

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DhiSure?

DhiSure is an insurance management software designed specifically for insurance brokers and agencies to manage client records, renewals, and agents.

Is DhiSure only for vehicle insurance?

DhiSure is primarily built for vehicle insurance workflows but can also support other insurance categories with similar requirements.

Can I import existing data?

Yes. DhiSure supports data import from spreadsheets and other systems, making migration simple and efficient.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. DhiSure is designed for insurance professionals and requires no technical expertise, coding, or complicated setup.

Can multiple assistants use it?

Yes. DhiSure supports multiple user accounts with role-based access control for teams and agency staff.

How is DhiSure better than Excel?

DhiSure provides structured workflows, automated renewal tracking, secure collaboration, and centralized records built specifically for insurance.
