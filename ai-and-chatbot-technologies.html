Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Everything you need to know about conversational AI implementations.

What is an AI chatbot?

An AI chatbot is a software application that uses artificial intelligence and NLP to simulate human-like conversations with users.

What is the difference between rule-based and AI chatbots?

Rule-based chatbots follow predefined scripts, while AI chatbots use machine learning and NLP to understand and respond dynamically.

What technologies are used in AI chatbots?

Technologies include NLP, machine learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), APIs, and cloud platforms.

What are real-world examples of AI chatbots?

Customer support bots, virtual assistants (Siri, Alexa), banking chatbots, and e-commerce virtual shoppers.

How do AI chatbots improve over time?

They learn from user interactions, feedback loops, sentiment analysis, and continuous NLP model training.

What is conversational AI?

Conversational AI refers to technologies that enable machines to understand, process, and respond to human language naturally.

What are the key components of a chatbot system?

An NLP engine, dialogue manager, integration APIs, knowledge base, and analytics monitoring systems.

What are the challenges in chatbot development?

Handling context, maintaining high accuracy, ensuring data privacy, and navigating highly complex or nuanced conversations.
