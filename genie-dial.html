Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a developer?

No. Genie Dial is designed to be set up in under 2 minutes with no coding required. Just connect your number and upload your business info.

Can it handle regional Indian languages?

Yes. Genie Dial supports 40+ languages including Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, and more.

What happens when a call is too complex?

Genie Dial automatically escalates complex or sensitive calls to your designated human agents — seamlessly, mid-conversation.

Is my customer data secure?

Absolutely. All calls and data are encrypted end-to-end. Genie Dial is built with enterprise-grade security and compliance standards.
