Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Genie Dial technologies and implementation.

Do I need a developer or technical team to set up Genie Dial?

No. Genie Dial is designed to be set up in under 2 minutes with no coding required. Just connect your number and upload your business info.

Can Genie Dial handle calls in regional Indian languages?

Yes. Genie Dial supports 40+ languages including Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, and more.

What happens when a call is too complex for the AI?

Genie Dial automatically escalates complex or sensitive calls to your designated human agents — seamlessly, mid-conversation.

Is my customer data secure?

Absolutely. All calls and data are encrypted end-to-end. Genie Dial is built with enterprise-grade security and compliance standards.

Can I use my existing business phone number?

Yes. You can link your current number to Genie Dial. No hardware changes or carrier switches required.

How does pricing work?

Genie Dial offers flexible plans based on call volume. Get early access to discuss a plan tailored to your business size and needs.
