About DhiHealth

Simplifying Hospital Operations with Smart Healthcare Technology

DhiHealth is a smart hospital management platform built for clinics, hospitals, nursing homes, and healthcare centres. It helps healthcare teams manage patient records, doctor schedules, appointments, and real-time ward updates from one centralized system.

By reducing manual work and improving coordination across departments, DhiHealth helps staff focus more on patient care and daily operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DhiHealth suitable for small clinics or only large hospitals?

DhiHealth is built for healthcare facilities of all sizes. Whether you run a single-doctor clinic or a multi-ward hospital, the platform scales to match your complexity without adding unnecessary overhead.

Does DhiHealth require any technical knowledge to set up?

No. DhiHealth is designed for healthcare administrators and medical staff, not IT professionals. Onboarding is guided, intuitive, and takes less than a day to get your facility fully operational.

Can we manage multiple departments and doctors in one account?

Yes. DhiHealth supports multi-department and multi-doctor setups under a single hospital workspace. Each department and doctor gets their own view while administrators maintain a complete facility-wide overview.

How does real-time ICU and ward monitoring work?

DhiHealth connects your ward and ICU data to a live dashboard and smart display system. Any status change — bed occupancy, patient transfer, or availability — is updated instantly and visible across the facility.

Are patient reminders sent automatically?

Yes. DhiHealth automatically sends appointment reminders, follow-up notifications, and medication alerts to patients at pre-set intervals. No manual follow-up calls required.

Is patient data secure in DhiHealth?

Absolutely. All patient records and hospital data are stored securely with role-based access control. Only authorised staff can access sensitive information, and data is never shared with third parties.
